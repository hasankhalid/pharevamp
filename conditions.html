Park Conditions

Section Overview

This visualization summarises surveys conducted by GAT Consulting to monitor general conditions of parks. To make this visualization, each park/ location was assigned a score based on the data collected in the surveys. This data was aggregated by data and zones and is presented in the visual that follows. Radius of the circles represents the number of locations reached on a particular day and the color is mapped to average condition score of the parks/ locations reached.
