Section Overview

The heatmaps in this section show the activity of enumerators over time. The calendar layout on the top shows count of surveys/ attendances conducted per day. On the other hand, the second layout shows the zones the activity was (predominantly) carried out. This tool can be used to assess overarching performance of PHA enumerators since the inception of the project.
